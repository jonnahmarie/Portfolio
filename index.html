About Me

My name is Jonnah Marie. I am a Los Angeles native. This past August, I recently moved back to California after nearly 3 years from New Zealand after having earned my Bachelor of Science in Zoology and Ecology there.

Despite this, I currently work as a graphic designer but hope to work my way into front end development. I have worked a wide range of jobs from entertainment payroll to veterinary assistance, however I quite enjoy the aspect of creating aesthetically pleasing visual web applications with plain text lines of code and am often inspired by beautiful web pages like they are works of art. This interest has led me to the decision of enrolling in the UCLA Coding Bootcamp.

My hobbies include knitting, baking, watching Korean drama shows, playing games, and reading. I thoroughly enjoy pop culture and am a fan of Harry Potter, Star Wars, The Lord of the Rings and more.
